About Me

I am a finance major turned accountant / data manager turned entrepreneur turned hit-hard-by-reality entrepreneur turned designer.

I’ve always been a “if you’re happy then I’m happy” kind of guy, which translates nicely to UX design. UI design and Front-End Development both have a place in my heart, but the process of getting to know the user and tailoring a design for them will always be where my passion lies.
 
Nicely landscaped grass and being outdoors relaxes me, so I pretend I can golf. Pizza tends to make my stomach ache, so my stomach aches often.
